Enjoy a happy exchange

The Exchange is a 15-seat intentional dining room in Chennai where food is the medium and conversation is the product. Every service is designed around warm hospitality, small-batch menus, and guided conversation cards that make it easy to share stories, beliefs, and memories with the people at your table.

Tamil-rooted, globally inspired food, fixed three-course menus, and just 15 seats per service to keep every exchange human and unhurried.

Menu
Fixed exchanges, small menu, big conversations

Our menu is intentionally compact, inspired by Tamil home-style comfort and crowd-favourite global dishes. Portions are limited each day, so once a dish is gone, it’s gone for that service.

Every exchange leaves something behind

A portion of our revenue – plus anything guests choose to add – goes into The Exchange CSR pool. Once a month, we direct that pool to causes chosen by our guests through a simple, playful vote.
